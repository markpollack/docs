## Mission

Pollack AI Lab is a personal research lab focused on one question: **what infrastructure makes AI agents actually reliable?**

Not bigger models. Not better prompts. Infrastructure — knowledge bases, structured execution, evaluation systems, and behavioral analysis.

## Who

**Mark Pollack** — Founder of [Spring AI](https://spring.io/projects/spring-ai), former VMware/Broadcom Principal Engineer, now building agent infrastructure at [Tuvium](https://tuvium.com) and Developer Advocate at JetBrains.

20+ years building developer frameworks (Spring .NET, Spring XD, Spring Cloud Data Flow). Now applying that same "infrastructure over cleverness" philosophy to AI agents.

## The Thesis

> **Knowledge + structured execution > model**

Agent reliability improves more from giving agents the right knowledge and constraining their execution than from switching to a larger model. We call this **knowledge-directed execution**.

This lab exists to build the tools that make that thesis tangible and run the experiments that prove it.
