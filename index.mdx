We build tools that help you understand what your agents are actually doing — and make them better. Every tool call leaves a trace. We capture those traces, score the output, and analyze the behavior. When something goes wrong, we can see *where* and *why*. Then we fix it and measure whether the fix worked.

**Mark Pollack** — creator of [Spring AI](https://spring.io/projects/spring-ai). Previously VMware/Broadcom (Spring team). Now building agent infrastructure and Developer Advocate at JetBrains.
