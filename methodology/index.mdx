## Experiment Protocol

Every experiment follows this structure:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Define hypothesis">
    What are we testing? One variable, controlled comparison.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Design variants">
    Progressive ladder from baseline to full treatment. Each variant adds exactly one thing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deterministic preprocessing">
    Parse inputs before the LLM sees them. Route to knowledge bases. Zero LLM cost for this stage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run N times">
    Each variant runs N=3+ times for statistical confidence. Full traces captured.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Evaluate with four-tier jury">
    T0 (deterministic) → T1 (command) → T2 (golden test) → T3 (LLM). Cheap filters first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Analyze behavioral traces">
    Build Markov chains from tool-call sequences. Compare transition probabilities across variants.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Experiment page here in the lab, narrative on blog.pollack.ai, raw data on GitHub.
  </Step>
</Steps>
