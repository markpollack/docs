Agento Studio is an interactive environment for creating autonomous agents on the JVM. It takes the build-measure-improve cycle that every serious agent project needs and wraps it in tooling that makes each step repeatable.

## What Makes It Different

Most agent platforms stop at observation — traces, logs, dashboards. Agento Studio starts there and keeps going: it scaffolds your project, builds your knowledge base, sets up your judges, and tracks improvement across runs. Create, measure, improve.
